Wembley Chemicals and Supplies Ltd. is a company mainly active in the international trading of petrochemical products such as glycols, absorbents, and feedstock for the production of petroleum resins and operating in the Russian Federation, Europe and many other countries worldwide.

about as

Relatively young and fast growing the Company was founded in 2011 and has already positioned itself as a reliable, responsible and flexible partner to the global petrochemical producers in the market.

Wembley Chemicals and Supplies Ltd. employs a team of skilled and high performing young specialists qualified in sales, logistics, finance and project management with more than 10 years of experience in the petrochemical industry.
